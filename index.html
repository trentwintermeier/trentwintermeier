Trent is a first-year doctoral student in the Department of Rhetoric and Writing at the University of Texas at Austin. Currently, he is a Graduate Research Assistant for the AudiAnnotate project, and he is a LLILAS Benson Digital Scholarship intern. His research interests include rhetorical theory, cultural rhetorics, science and technology studies, western philosophy, and the digital humanities.
